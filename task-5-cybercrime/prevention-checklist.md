**🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**



* Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
* Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests
* Always verify the sender before making transactions
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
* Use official apps only from trusted sources
* Enable two-factor authentication on accounts
* Regularly monitor bank transactions



**💰 UPI-Specific Tips:**



* Remember: Receiving money does NOT require approval

&#x20;            Never scan unknown QR codes for payment
