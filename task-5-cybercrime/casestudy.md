###### &#x20;**Case Study: UPI / Online Payment Fraud** 



One of the most common cybercrimes that affects students and young individuals in India is UPI fraud. UPI fraud is a type of cybercrime where a person is tricked into revealing sensitive information related to online transactions or is tricked into sending money to fraudsters.



One such scenario is when a person receives a call or message stating that they have to ‘verify’ their account or receive money. The person on the other end is a fraudster and tricks the person into sending money to them. However, in this case, the person is tricked into sending money to the fraudster instead of receiving it.



Another way in which this type of cybercrime is committed is through fake links or QR codes that redirect users to phishing sites and trick them into revealing sensitive information related to online transactions.



Students are often targeted because they make extensive use of online payment apps and may not be aware of this type of cybercrime.



The consequences of this type of cybercrime include not only monetary losses and mental stress but also identity theft. In some cases, individuals may even lose access to their accounts.
