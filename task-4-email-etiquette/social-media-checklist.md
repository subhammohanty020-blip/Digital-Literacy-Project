**📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist**



**✅ Do’s:**



* Maintain a professional profile (username, bio, posts)
* Think before posting anything publicly
* Respect others’ opinions online
* Utilize privacy settings properly
* Share useful and authentic content





**❌ Don’ts:**



* Do not share personal information with others online
* Avoid posting offensive and inappropriate content online
* Do not get into any argument or start “trolling” others online
* Do not spread any misinformation online
* Do not use someone else’s pictures and data
